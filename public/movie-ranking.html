The Best Movies According to Carlos

My top 3 movies of all-time in no particular order.

The Dark Knight

My favorite film director together with one of my favorite actors and one of the best music composers, cinematographic masterpiece

Whiplash

Superb performances by JK Simmons and Miles Teller. Really tense and inspiring movie

Interstellar

Emotional tearjerker. The outstanding performance by Matthew McConaughey is what takes it to a whole new level
